Locals Do It / Discover Nansensgade

A guide to an elevated experience in one of the coziest and most cool streets in Copenhagen. Personal recommendations on what to do and explore from the people who know the street better than anyone: the locals.

The guide is designed to make it easy and inspiring to explore Nansensgade — whether you are here for the first time or already know the area well. Along the way, you are invited to experience the street at your own pace, with a focus on atmosphere, inspiration, and the small moments that unfold between shops, cafés, and cultural spaces.

As part of the concept, each shop featured in the guide offers a summer treat that can be redeemed directly in the street. The brochure can be purchased in all participating shops for 75 DKK and can be redeemed throughout the summer.

Enjoy your visit.
